## üß† Core Problem-Solving Strategies

### 1. **Sliding Window**
- **Use case:** Arrays or strings where you need to find subarrays/substrings of fixed or variable length.
- **Examples:** Maximum sum subarray, longest substring without repeating characters.

### 2. **Two Pointers**
- **Use case:** Sorted arrays or strings for pair comparisons, merging, or partitioning.
- **Examples:** Merge sorted arrays, remove duplicates, palindrome check.

### 3. **Binary Search**
- **Use case:** Sorted arrays or monotonic functions.
- **Examples:** Search in sorted array, find square root, peak element.

### 4. **Prefix Sum / Difference Array**
- **Use case:** Fast range queries or cumulative computations.
- **Examples:** Subarray sum, range update, matrix sum queries.

### 5. **Greedy Algorithms**
- **Use case:** Optimization problems where local choices lead to global optimum.
- **Examples:** Activity selection, coin change (greedy version), Huffman coding.

### 6. **Divide and Conquer**
- **Use case:** Problems that can be broken into smaller subproblems.
- **Examples:** Merge sort, quick sort, binary search.

### 7. **Dynamic Programming (DP)**
- **Use case:** Overlapping subproblems with optimal substructure.
- **Examples:** Fibonacci, knapsack, longest common subsequence.

### 8. **Backtracking**
- **Use case:** Decision trees, permutations, combinations.
- **Examples:** N-Queens, Sudoku solver, subset generation.

### 9. **Recursion**
- **Use case:** Problems naturally defined in terms of smaller versions.
- **Examples:** Tree traversals, factorial, DFS.

### 10. **Memoization**
- **Use case:** Optimize recursive solutions by caching results.
- **Examples:** Fibonacci, climbing stairs, DP problems.

## üîç Search & Traversal Strategies

### 11. **Breadth-First Search (BFS)**
- **Use case:** Shortest path in unweighted graphs, level-order traversal.
- **Examples:** Maze solving, word ladder, tree levels.

### 12. **Depth-First Search (DFS)**
- **Use case:** Explore all paths, detect cycles, connected components.
- **Examples:** Graph traversal, island counting, topological sort.

### 13. **Topological Sorting**
- **Use case:** Ordering tasks with dependencies (DAG).
- **Examples:** Course scheduling, build systems.

### 14. **Union-Find (Disjoint Set)**
- **Use case:** Track connected components efficiently.
- **Examples:** Cycle detection, Kruskal‚Äôs algorithm.
